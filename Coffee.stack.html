I'm drinking tea.
Sorry to disappoint anyone who went to this stack expecting a story about drinking coffee. 
It began when I was playing Go.

I was also playing dice.
There was no reason to, but I added marshmallows to my tea. They softened the flavor, but seemed silly.
The plastic stirring stick went around, and around, and around, and around....
That was when I really wanted some cheese. 
I'm a cheese dog. I mean, if Buddha decides to reincarnate me, I'm going to be taught a lesson. It's that much of a problem. So I went to the cheese vault and tapped in R-Q-F-R-T just like I always do. I decided to come out with a few pounds of bleu, which I would mix with garden fresh berries, to make my lunch shake.



I took the train back to Dusseldorf and followed the queue to "Returning Heroes of Interstellar Defense Force". There's a story there, for sure -- but focus on the cheese.
I'm late for my job. I ring the door bell at Walmart whenever someone wants to come in. It's only 66 hours so I don't get benefits, but I get two pay-checks for also being the one that welcomes them inside. 

Service with a smile, that's me.
Finally I was done. 
Done with the cheese, and done telling you a story.
